# Part A｜抽象母版

## A1. 总母版（中文）
```text
一张具有玄光美学气质的视觉作品：幽暗、空寂、辽阔的世界中，一束神性微光照见孤独主体。整体风格融合东方留白、低清颗粒、柔和光晕、诗性氛围与克制的神秘感。画面强调空灵、宿命、寂静与高级感，不依赖复杂元素堆砌，而依赖光、留白、孤独主体与微妙情绪建立感染力。
```

## A1. Master Abstract Prompt (EN)
```text
A visual work in the Xuanlight Aesthetic: within a dark, spacious, and quiet world, a sacred subtle light reveals a solitary subject. The overall style blends oriental negative space, low-resolution grain, soft halation, poetic atmosphere, and restrained mystery. The image should feel lonely, sublime, still, and high-end, relying not on clutter but on light, emptiness, and emotional concentration.
```

# Part B｜通用母版

## B1. 总通用版（中文）
```text
以玄光美学方式创作一张图：主体置于空旷幽暗的环境中，被一种神性天光、月轮、星轮或灵核般的光源照见。画面具有留白、低清颗粒、轻微失焦、柔和 bloom、胶片噪点与清冷色调。整体气质应空灵、克制、孤独、诗性，可以融合东方水墨、仙侠意境、数据灵魂感、废墟感或梦境叙事，但必须保持高级与简洁。
```

## B1. Generic Master Prompt (EN)
```text
Create an image in the Xuanlight Aesthetic: place the subject in a dark, spacious, quiet environment, illuminated by a sacred celestial light, moonlike disc, star wheel, or glowing soul-core. The image should feature negative space, low-resolution grain, subtle blur, soft bloom, film noise, and a cool restrained palette. The mood should be lonely, poetic, restrained, and sublime, while optionally blending oriental ink-wash, xianxia atmosphere, soulcore sci-fi, ruins, or dreamlike interior storytelling.
```

The overall result should feel restrained and poetic, like cinematic still frames.

# Part D｜具体任务模板

## D1. 当前风格的东方水墨极简版（中文）
```text
一张极简的玄光美学东方插画：幽暗而辽阔的天地之间，主体被一束神性微光照见。画面可以是人，也可以是空景、器物、建筑、意识体或孤独存在，不限定为女剑客。整体以东方水墨与留白为基底，加入低清颗粒、轻微失焦、柔和 bloom 与 halation，颜色以月白、雾蓝、墨灰、深靛蓝为主，辅以极少量淡金或冷白高光。画面务必克制、疏朗、安静，不繁琐，不堆叠，用“光、空、静、孤”建立感染力。
```

## D1. Oriental Minimal Xuanlight (EN)
```text
A minimalist oriental image in the Xuanlight Aesthetic: within a dark and spacious world, the subject is revealed by a sacred subtle light. The subject may be a person, an empty scene, an object, a structure, a consciousness-form, or any solitary presence; it is not limited to a female swordsman. The image should be grounded in oriental ink-wash and negative space, with low-resolution grain, slight blur, soft bloom, and halation. Use a palette of moon-white, mist-blue, ink-gray, and deep indigo, with only tiny amounts of pale gold or cool white highlights. Keep the composition restrained, sparse, quiet, and uncluttered, building emotional force through light, emptiness, stillness, and solitude.
```

# Part F｜参数建议

## Midjourney
```text
--ar 1:1 / 4:5 / 9:16 / 16:9
--stylize 300-700
--chaos 4-12
```

建议：
- 越极简，chaos 越低
- 越梦境 / 废墟 / 灵核，stylize 可稍高
- 若画漫画锚点图，先用较低 chaos 稳定角色

## SDXL / Flux 类
```text
Steps: 24-40
CFG: 4.5-7
Denoise: 0.3-0.55（视工作流而定）
```

建议：
- 不要过度锐化
- 保留颗粒与柔光
- 若需要高级感，宁可略虚，不要过于干净
