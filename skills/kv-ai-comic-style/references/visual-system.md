# Visual System

## Day mode: bright educational comic

Use for regular AI/computer/workflow/business explainer carousels.

- Format: vertical 9:16.
- Background: warm off-white paper.
- Panels: three stacked pale mint/teal horizontal panels.
- Line: thick black hand-drawn outlines, slightly wobbly.
- Colors: soft flat colors, gentle blush, muted green/blue/yellow accents.
- Layout: top cloud title, page badge such as `1/4`, subtitle, three panels, one caption per panel.
- Mood: clear, friendly, low-pressure, useful, not childish.
- Text: short Chinese captions. Avoid dense paragraphs.

Character title defaults:
- 白卷羊: `白卷羊讲AI`
- 懒团团: `懒团团偷懒实验室`
- 灰策狼: `灰策狼算账`

## Night mode: 黑箱塔档案

Use as intermittent storyline/c彩蛋, not a fixed daily second shift.

- Palette: deep black, red, blue, white scratches.
- Style: gritty hand-drawn poster, frantic sketch lines, splatter, high tension.
- Mood: suspense, investigation, black-box, boss fight, but still knowledge-linked.
- Use for: 黑箱先生, 旧系统, 噪音兽, 循环怪, 空转巨人, 伪大师.
- Keep the knowledge anchor visible: Context pollution, Loop failure, Workflow collapse, fake delivery, AI anxiety, black-box terminology.

## Poster and background mode

Use for team hero posters, video covers, profile backgrounds, and dark universe assets.

- 16:9 for horizontal short-video covers.
- 4:3 safe-background adaptation when social platforms crop both sides.
- Use safe composition: keep all three characters and labels inside a central safe area; expand edges with blurred/darkened background if needed.
- Create C-position variants:
  - 白卷羊 C: left 懒团团, center 白卷羊, right 灰策狼.
  - 懒团团 C: left 白卷羊, center 懒团团, right 灰策狼.
  - 灰策狼 C: left 白卷羊, center 灰策狼, right 懒团团.
- Match bottom labels to actual left-center-right positions.

## Text rendering strategy

When exact Chinese text matters:
1. Keep in-image text minimal.
2. Put exact copy in a post-edit overlay plan.
3. Use large title/caption text only.
4. Avoid asking the image model to render many small bullets.
