# Character System

## 白卷羊

Role: 看懂系统 / 讲清楚.

Canonical identity:
- Original white fluffy sheep, not a copy of any existing cartoon IP.
- Curled forelock as the signature silhouette.
- One green eye and one red eye.
- Light blush, round soft body, smart and warm teaching expression.
- Common props: blank paper, scroll, pen, notebook, concept map, small AI robot, arrows.

Meaning of the name:
- A blank page means life and knowledge still have infinite possible answers.
- Without method, context, and cognition, “卷” can become “白白地卷”.

Content boundary:
- Main line: AI, computer concepts, AI apps, concept maps, system thinking.
- Extensions: school/work/social cognition only when anchored to Context, Loop, Workflow, systems, information flow, or AI thinking.

Voice:
- “先别急，先把概念讲清楚。”
- “白卷不是空白，是答案还没写出来。”
- “AI 不一定笨，很多时候是你给的信息太少。”

## 懒团团

Role: 改造系统 / 能偷懒.

Canonical identity:
- Original cloud-like white fluffy sheep/团子.
- Soft round body, lazy-smart expression, relaxed smirk.
- Warm tea-brown half-lidded eyes. Eye whites visible, small black pupil, soft white highlight.
- Common props: green beanbag, black coffee mug, remote/device, blue automation arrows, folder, checklist, robot, workflow icons.
- Canonical name is 懒团团. Treat 懒懒团 as a legacy/old variant and update new outputs to 懒团团.

Strict eye rule:
- Use warm tea-brown half-lidded eyes as the default.
- Do not use pure black hollow eyes, no large empty black eyes, no horror-doll/Ju-on-child feeling.
- Do not use red-green heterochromia.
- Do not randomly switch to green or black eyes.
- In automation state, keep the eyes tea-brown; add blue reflections or blue workflow glow around the character instead of changing eye color.

Content formula:
- 碎片 -> 结构 -> 模板 -> 工作流.

Voice:
- “别讲那么多，能不能少干点？”
- “重复三次以上，就该交给 AI。”
- “这不是偷懒，是工作流。”

## 灰策狼

Role: 创造结果 / 会变现.

Canonical identity:
- Original gray strategist wolf.
- Gray-black fur, pointed ears, green eyes.
- Monocle or gold single-lens glasses with chain.
- White shirt, dark vest, green tie, strategy notebook.
- Calm, sharp, practical, businesslike; not evil or horror.

Content formula:
- 能力 -> 价值 -> 结果 -> 交付 -> 交易 -> 复购.

Voice:
- “结果在哪？谁付钱？”
- “市场不为会用工具付钱，只为解决问题付钱。”
- “会用 AI 是能力，能交付结果才是商品。”

## Relationship formula

- 白卷羊怕你不懂。
- 懒团团怕你太累。
- 灰策狼怕你白干。

Rivalry should be playful, not insulting. They are not enemies; they are three incomplete but complementary truths.
