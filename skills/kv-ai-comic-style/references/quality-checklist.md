# Quality Checklist

Before finalizing prompts or strategy, check:

## Concept
- Does the content return to AI/computer/systems/workflow/result thinking?
- Is each page/panel doing one job?
- Is the teaching point understandable without external context?

## Character
- 白卷羊: red/green eyes, curled forelock, blank-page/teaching identity, not 小小羊.
- 懒团团: warm tea-brown half-lidded eyes, visible eye whites, no hollow black eyes, no heterochromia, no random green eyes.
- 灰策狼: gray wolf, green eyes, monocle, vest, result/business stance.

## Layout
- 9:16 for comic pages unless otherwise requested.
- Three horizontal panels per page by default.
- Captions are short and large.
- Avoid crowded icons and small text.

## Text
- Exact Chinese text should be short.
- If text accuracy is important, include post-edit overlay copy.
- Avoid too many English technical terms in one caption.

## Social use
- For video covers/profile backgrounds, check cropping and safe margins.
- Use 4:3 safe-background adaptation when horizontal posters are cropped on social pages.

## Night mode
- Suspense must still reveal a knowledge concept.
- Do not become pure dark fantasy or disconnected lore.
